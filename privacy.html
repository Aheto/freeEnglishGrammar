Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mini OpenStax. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our educational web application.

Mini OpenStax is designed to be a lightweight, privacy-first educational tool. We believe that learning should be accessible without compromising user data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store your personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device using your web browser's localStorage feature.

The information stored locally on your device includes:

  • Student Name: Used to personalize your dashboard and quiz reports.
  • User Role: To toggle between Student and Instructor views.
  • Quiz Attempts & Mastery Status: To track your progress across lessons and days.
  • Quiz Reports: Detailed breakdowns of your quiz scores, correct/incorrect answers, and time spent.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used solely to enhance your learning experience:

  • Personalize your dashboard and welcome messages.
  • Track your academic progress and mastery of specific grammar topics.
  • Generate detailed quiz reports for your records and review.
  • Allow instructors (if you switch roles) to view class analytics based on local data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • WhatsApp Integration: If you choose to share your quiz reports via WhatsApp, the app will use the WhatsApp API (wa.me) to pre-fill a message with your report data. This data is then transmitted via WhatsApp's service, which is subject to Meta's own privacy policies.
  • Google Fonts & Font Awesome: We use these third-party services to load fonts and icons for a better visual experience. Their respective privacy policies apply to the loading of these resources.

5. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security is tied to the security of your browser and device.

Important: Clearing your browser's cache, cookies, or local storage will permanently delete your progress, quiz attempts, and reports. We recommend regularly exporting or sharing your reports via WhatsApp or PDF if you wish to keep a permanent backup.

6. Children's Privacy

Mini OpenStax is an educational platform designed for learners of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect personal information on external servers and all data remains locally on the user's device, we do not knowingly contact or collect data from children in a way that violates standard data protection regulations. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's device usage and help them manage their local browser data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
