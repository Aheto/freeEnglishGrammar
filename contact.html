Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform and services.

How do I register for a class?

You can easily register by clicking the "Send via WhatsApp" button above or by calling us directly at +233 25 732 0201. Our admissions team will guide you through the simple enrollment process and help you select the right course.

Is the content aligned with the NaCCA curriculum?

Yes! All our educational materials, including the Class 3 and Class 4 English Language modules, are strictly aligned with the Ghana National Council for Curriculum and Assessment (NaCCA) standards-based curriculum.

Can I track my child's or student's progress?

Absolutely. Parents and instructors can switch to "Instructor Mode" in the Dashboard settings to view detailed analytics, quiz reports, and mastery tracking for the learners. Reports can also be shared directly via WhatsApp.

Do I need an internet connection to use the platform?

Our web app is designed to be highly lightweight. While an initial internet connection is required to load the site, your progress, quiz attempts, and reports are saved locally on your device using browser storage.

Is Mini OpenStax free to use?

We offer free access to our core educational content and lesson plans. For personalized tutoring, live classes, and advanced analytics, we offer affordable registration packages. Contact us for current pricing.
